Tips for a Great Experience

We want everyone to have the best time possible! Here are a few small things that help our gatherings run smoothly:
Settle in: Feel free to hop on a few minutes early to say hi and get comfortable.
Sound Check: We recommend keeping your mic muted when you aren't speaking to help keep our chat clear for everyone.
Camera Ready: If you're planning to dance, try to position your camera so we can see your lovely movements.
Just Enjoy: Most importantly, relax, have fun, and don't hesitate to jump in with questions or comments!
